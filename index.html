Menstrual Products
▶
PadsWorn outside the body. Great for beginners. Change every 4–6 hours.
TamponsInserted internally. Use lowest absorbency needed. Change every 4–8 hours.
Menstrual CupReusable silicone cup. Eco-friendly. Can wear up to 12 hours.
Period UnderwearAbsorbent underwear. Comfortable and reusable. Great for light days.
DiscsWorn at the cervix. Can be worn during intimacy. Holds more than a cup.
🌙
Cycle Phases
▶
🔴 Menstrual (Days 1–5)Uterine lining sheds. Rest, warmth & iron-rich foods help.
🌱 Follicular (Days 6–13)Energy rises. Estrogen builds up. Great time for new projects.
✨ Ovulation (Day 14)Egg released. Peak energy & confidence. You may feel your best!
🍂 Luteal (Days 15–28)Progesterone rises then drops. PMS can occur. Self-care is key.
💚
PMS Basics
▶
What is PMS?Premenstrual Syndrome — physical & emotional symptoms in the luteal phase (days 15–28).
Common SymptomsBloating, breast tenderness, mood swings, fatigue, cravings, headaches.
Nutrition HelpReduce sodium & sugar. Add magnesium-rich foods (dark chocolate, leafy greens).
MovementLight exercise like walking or yoga can reduce bloating and improve mood.
SleepAim for 7–9 hours. Your body heals during rest, especially during this phase.
🩺
When to See a Doctor
▶
Very Heavy BleedingSoaking a pad/tampon every hour for 2+ hours. Could indicate fibroids or a clotting issue.
Severe CrampsPain that disrupts daily life may be endometriosis or adenomyosis — seek evaluation.
Irregular CyclesCycles consistently shorter than 21 or longer than 35 days warrant a check-up.
Missed PeriodsMissing 3+ periods (excluding pregnancy) may signal hormonal or thyroid issues.
Unusual DischargeStrong odor, unusual color, or itching outside of period can indicate infection.
